Founded

The Austin AI Exchange brings together the people doing the actual work — building, deploying, governing, and rethinking workflows with AI. No vendor pitches. No vague futurism. Just practitioners sharing what they've learned, what's broken, and what's working in the open.

We were founded in 2024 by a small group of finance, engineering, and operations leaders who kept ending up at the same coffee shop, comparing notes on what their teams were really shipping. The conversations got too good to keep private.

Today the Exchange operates as a community-first nonprofit, hosting monthly gatherings and offering consulting engagements that fund the community's work.
